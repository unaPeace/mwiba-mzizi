My professional History.

I got my first paying project during my high school years. The assignment I was tasked with was to edit architectural drawings for a freelance draftsman who was working on council drawings for a client looking to build a house in Pretoria.

Out of sheer curiosity and a willingness to learn, I engaged in diverse projects ranging from Architecture, design, content and media, marketing and more. I did all this while studying.

In 2018 friends of mine had the idea to develop an app called Konvi. They employed me to design the apps UI. Prior to this I had no experience working in tech. The experience I acquired served as the motivation to pursue coding as a profession.
